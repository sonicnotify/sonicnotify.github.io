Read about the new 4.0 Sonic Notify? If you have you know it all about Channels. Channels are the things ( both physical and software) which output the Sonic data. Some of our channels actually require some building of hardware!!!!

And Lev spent the day putting the finish touches on the Sonic Mini Broadcaster shipment materials which clients will be receiving with their units. Lots of pictures of hardware.
